    Your Confirmation Code

    Your Spirit Confirmation Code is a 6 character alphanumeric combination, for example ABC123.
    Please select where you booked and then read about where your Confirmation Code can be located.

    If you booked with a third party not listed below, your confirmation email from them should contain a 6 character code that may be referred to as a Confirmation Code or record locator.

    • Spirit
    • Expedia
    • Priceline
    • Cheapoair
    • Orbitz

    Well played, you – coming straight to spirit.com for our ultra-low fares!

    You'll find your Confirmation Code clearly highlighted near the top of your confirmation email that we sent shortly after booking, or directly beneath the "Check-In Online" button on your check-in reminder email.

    On Expedia's confirmation email, you'll find your 6 character Confirmation Code underneath the word "Confirmed" in the blue bar that highlights your flight itinerary.

    Remember, the itinerary number listed at the top of your email cannot be used to access your reservation at Spirit.com

    On Priceline's confirmation email, you'll find your 6 character Confirmation Code in the Airline Confirmation Numbers section, directly below the Flight Numbers section.

    Remember, the trip number shown at the top of your email cannot be used to access your reservation at Spirit.com

    On CheapOair's confirmation email, you'll find your 6 character Confirmation Code further down in the Flight Summary section under "Airline Confirmation".

    Remember, the booking number shown at the top of your email cannot be used to access your reservation at Spirit.com

    On Orbitz's confirmation email, you'll find your 6 character Confirmation Code directly below the "Record Locator" heading at the top of the email.

    Remember, neither the Airline Ticket Number or Orbitz Record Locator number shown later in your email can be used to access your reservation at Spirit.com
