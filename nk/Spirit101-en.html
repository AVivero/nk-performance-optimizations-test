Spirit gives you More Go for less money

As home of the Bare Fare®, our low ticket prices are designed to save you an average of 30% more than other airlines.* That means you and a personal item can get from Point A to B as economically as possible by paying only for the things you need or want. Some call it à la carte, but we like to call it à la smarte. *Based on data from the Department of Transportation and verified by an independent service
