About Spirit
About Us

Spirit Airlines (NASDAQ: SAVE) is committed to offering the lowest total price to the places we fly, on average much lower than other airlines. We start you off with an unbundled Bare Fare™.  Then you get Frill Control™ which allows you to pay only for the options you choose - like bags, seat assignments and refreshments - the things other airlines bake right into their ticket prices. We help people save money and travel more often, create new jobs and stimulate business growth in the communities we serve. With our Fit Fleet™, the youngest fleet of any major U.S. airline, we operate more than 400 daily flights to 60 destinations in the U.S., Latin America and the Caribbean.

Media note: Aircraft photos and video B-roll are available in the Media Center.
