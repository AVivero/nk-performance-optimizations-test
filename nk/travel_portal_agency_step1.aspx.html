Agency Registration

You can open a Travel Agency account with us in 2 easy steps. Begin by filling in your organization details below as well as the details of the primary agent, who will serve as the administrator of your account.

Once your registration is complete and we have activated your account, you will be able to register additional agents by completing the Agent Registration form.

If you have any questions, please contact us at agencysupport@spirit.com.
